Welcome to Company Event Management

I originally wrote this event management in PHP couple years ago when working on a project.
Here is a very simplified version of it I decided to re-write using Spring boot.

A multinational company (let call it Tech) have many Subsidiaries around the word.

  • Company designates here one Subsidiary
  • Country designates a country where one Subsidiary is located
  • User designates an employee
  • Attendee is an employee of any Subsidiary who attend an event
  • Lead designates a future customer of Tech company
  • Event type can be either a conference, an exhibition or a meeting
  • Event can take place in any country where the company Tech is present or can be online depending on the event type.
